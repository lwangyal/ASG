About Our Clinic

Dedicated to restoring balance and health.

At Ayushi Sorig Gatsal Clinic, we believe healthcare is more than just treating symptoms; it is about treating the person. With over 10 years of experience, our team is certified in traditional Tibetan holistic wellness therapies. We provide holistic treatment based on Sowa Rigpa (Traditional Tibetan Medicine), using diet guidance, lifestyle advice, herbal medicines, and therapies like medicinal baths and massage to restore body balance and support conditions such as arthritis, diabetes, and digestive disorders.
